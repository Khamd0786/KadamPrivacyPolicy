1. Introduction

Welcome to Kadam, a fitness tracking and social sharing application developed by Hammad Khan ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use the Kadam app, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and the choices you have.

By creating an account or using Kadam, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

Kadam is a free app. We do not run advertisements, sell your personal data to third parties, or share your information with data brokers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account & Profile Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Display name and username
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Short bio / description (optional)
  • Account creation date
  • Authentication tokens (access and refresh tokens, stored encrypted on your device)

You may sign up with an email and password or via Google Sign-In. For Google Sign-In, we receive your name and email from Google; we do not receive your Google password.

2.2 Location Data

Location is only collected while you are actively recording an activity. We do not track your location in the background or when the app is closed.

During an active recording session, we collect:

  • GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude, altitude) approximately every 3 seconds
  • Timestamps for each GPS point
  • Start and end location addresses (reverse-geocoded)
  • Encoded route polyline for map display

This location data is used to calculate distance, pace, speed, elevation, and to display your activity route on a map.

2.3 Health & Fitness Data

Kadam records and stores the following workout data:

  • Sport type (Run, Ride, Walk, Hike)
  • Distance, duration (moving time and elapsed time)
  • Average and maximum speed, pace
  • Elevation gain, elevation loss, max and min elevation
  • Calories burned (estimated)
  • Heart rate (average and maximum, if your device provides this via sensor)
  • Cadence (if your device provides this)
  • Per-kilometer split data
  • Perceived effort level you enter
  • Tags you add to an activity

2.4 Photos & Media

  • Profile photos you upload
  • Photos you attach to activities

Media is stored on Firebase Storage (Google Cloud). Images are compressed before upload. We do not read EXIF metadata from your photos.

2.5 Social & Interaction Data

  • Users you follow and users who follow you
  • Kudos (likes) you give or receive on activities
  • Comments you write or receive
  • Users you have blocked
  • Reports you submit about activities or other users

2.6 Notifications Data

We store a device push notification token to send you in-app notifications for follows, kudos, and comments. You can disable notifications at any time in Settings.

2.7 App Preferences & Settings

  • Theme preference (light / dark / system)
  • Language preference
  • Notification enabled/disabled setting
  • Last selected sport type
  • Biometric authentication preference
  • Onboarding completion status

2.8 Usage & Technical Data

We collect limited technical information required for the app to function, including:

  • API request logs (server-side, for debugging)
  • App version and operating system version
  • Network error information (to diagnose connectivity issues)

We do not currently use third-party analytics or crash reporting SDKs (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics) that would profile your in-app behavior.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 Public Activities

Activities you post with Public visibility are visible to all Kadam users, including users you haven't followed. This includes your display name, profile photo, activity stats, route map (if you enable it), and photos. You can set any activity to Private before or after posting to restrict visibility.

4.2 Service Providers (Subprocessors)

We use the following providers to operate Kadam. They process data on our behalf and are contractually bound to protect it:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Storage) — account management and media storage
  • Google Play Services / Maps SDK — location services and map display
  • Push notification delivery service — delivering follow / kudos / comment notifications

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Kadam, its users, or the public.

4.4 Business Transfer

If Kadam is acquired, merged, or its assets are transferred, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data is subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

Account Deletion

You can delete your Kadam account at any time from Settings → Delete Account. Upon requesting deletion:

  • Your account is immediately deactivated and your profile is hidden from other users.
  • Your data is permanently purged from our servers within 30 days of the deletion request.
  • Cached copies in backup systems may persist for up to 90 days before being overwritten.
  • Content you posted publicly (activities, comments) is removed along with your account.

You may also contact us at khamd0786@gmail.com to request deletion of specific data without deleting your account.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encrypted connections.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in an encrypted DataStore (Android Jetpack) on your device.
  • Access tokens have a short expiry; refresh tokens are rotated on use.
  • Media files are stored in Firebase Storage with access controls.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to using industry-standard practices.

If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at khamd0786@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Kadam is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has created an account on Kadam, please contact us at khamd0786@gmail.com and we will promptly delete the account and associated data.

By using Kadam, you represent that you are at least 13 years of age.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Access & Correction

You can view and edit your profile information (name, username, bio, profile photo) at any time from your profile settings.

Activity Visibility

Each activity can be set to Public or Private. Private activities are only visible to you.

Notifications

You can enable or disable push notifications in Settings. You can also manage notifications through your device's system settings.

Block & Report

  • Block any user to prevent them from seeing your content and from interacting with you.
  • Report activities or users that violate our community guidelines.
  • View and manage your blocked users list in Settings → Blocked Users.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Delete Account. See Section 5 for the data purge timeline.

Data Requests

To request a copy of the data we hold about you, or to request deletion of specific data, contact us at khamd0786@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
